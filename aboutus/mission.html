"Gar Relief has a direct and meaningful effect on the children, sick and elderly of this remote and impoverished region in Nangchen."

- Ven. Dorzin Dhondrup Rinpoche
Founder, Gar Relief

Gar Relief is a US-based charitable non-profit organization dedicated to supporting grassroots projects for people living in dire poverty in remote areas in Nangchen County. Our mission is to build local capacity and improve quality of life by advancing access to education and primary health care and promoting self-sustainability projects. Currently, our work is focused in Tajuk village and surrounding areas in Nangchen.

Here's What We Do

Provide a free elementary school education to young nomadic children
Maintain a low-cost health clinic for residents who previously had to travel long distances for basic care
Deliver food, clothing and basic medical care to the most needy, destitute elders
Support basic infrastructure projects to improve quality of life


Gar Relief has applied for 501(c)(3) status and is currently awaiting a favorable outcome.
