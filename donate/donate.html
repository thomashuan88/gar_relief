"Your support will be used in the most virtuous way to benefit the many impoverished and forgotten beings one the world's rooftop. I believe that if we work hard together and in the right direction, the effects will spread like light."

Founder, Gar Relief

Gar Relief is a charitable non-profit organization established in 2012 in New Mexico USA by Venerable Dorzin Dhondrup Rinpoche to address the many needs of people living on the Tibetan Plateau. We have applied for 501(c)(3) status and are currently awaiting a favorable outcome. Click here to learn more about the needs of the people we serve.
